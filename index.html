☢ Austrian Radiation

This map displays the current background radiation levels across Austria. The data comes from 111 of the Austrian Strahlenfrühwarnsystem (radiation early warning system) measurement stations.

Don't be alarmed, for comparison eating one banana gives you a 98nSv dose. One dental x-ray is equivalent to around a 7500nSv.
